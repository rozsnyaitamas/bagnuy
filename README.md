# bagnuy

The world of bagnuy looks like a 2D world, but in fact it is a world shaped like a tube or dognut.

Half of the land is under water. Other parts of the earth are made of earth, and stones.

The world is populated by two types of life forms. Plants and Animals. Plants can not move, but they reproduce by just spreading to nearby places or dropping fruit.

The animals can move and need to eat and sleep. There are animals that eat other animals also. 

There is an intelligent animal, that will follow the player and help him if dor example, the player gives him a gift.
